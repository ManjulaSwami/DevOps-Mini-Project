Yoga Poses

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

This pose improves balance and stability in the legs, strengthens the thighs, calves, ankles and spine, and stretches the groins and inner thighs.

Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)

It stretches and strengthens the thighs, knees, and ankles. It also improves digestion and helps reduce stress and anxiety.

Prasarita Padottanasana (Forward Bend)

Navasana (Boat Pose)

This pose strengthens the abdominal muscles, hip flexors, and spine. It also stimulates the kidneys, thyroid and intestines.

Gomukhasana (Cow Face Pose)

This pose stretches the shoulders, chest, arms, hips and thighs. It improves posture and increases lung capacity.
